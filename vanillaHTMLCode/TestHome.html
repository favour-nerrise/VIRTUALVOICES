OUR PURPOSE

Our goal is to enable you to enhance your relationship with your special-needs sibling, while creating a community you can both feel a part of.

Having a sibling with autism doesn't mean that you can't have fun together. You'll just need to find a different way to interact, and we're here to help you along the way!
